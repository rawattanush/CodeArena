// Core Features

            

                 

                    

                    
Private Hubs

                    
Create invite-only leaderboards for your community. Track progress in a focused, competitive environment.

                

                

                    

                    
Performance Analytics

                    
Visualize rating history, submission stats, and solve-patterns with a personalized dashboard.

                

                

                    

                    
Community Driven

                    
Foster friendly rivalries. Compare stats, race to solve problems, and grow together as a team.

                

            

        

    

    
    

        

            
// Built For Your Community

            

                

                    
University Coding Clubs

                    
Foster a culture of competitive programming on campus. Run internal contests, track member progress, and identify top performers for ICPC and other competitions.

                    
Company Tech Teams

                    
Level up your engineering team's problem-solving skills. Use CodeArena for internal training, hiring assessments, or just friendly competition.

                     
Groups of Friends

                    
Challenge your friends, stay motivated, and learn together. Settle the debate of who the best coder is, once and for all.
